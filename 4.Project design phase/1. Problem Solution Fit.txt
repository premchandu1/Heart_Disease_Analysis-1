PROBLEM–SOLUTION FIT — HEART DISEASE ANALYSIS

1. The Problem
Heart disease continues to be one of the leading causes of death globally.  
People often lack awareness of how their lifestyle, physical activity, mental health, and habits (such as smoking or drinking) affect their heart condition.  

Traditional diagnosis requires:
- Medical checkups
- Blood tests
- Doctor consultations  
This makes prediction expensive, time-consuming, and not easily accessible.

There is a need for:
- A quick, accessible, data-driven risk assessment tool.
- A dashboard that visually explains the main risk factors.

2. Who Faces the Problem?
- Adults aged 18–80+
- People with lifestyle-risk patterns (smoking, alcohol, low sleep)
- Individuals with high BMI, diabetes, stroke history
- Patients wanting early detection before symptoms worsen
- Healthcare workers needing fast preliminary assessment tools

3. Why Existing Methods Are Not Enough?
- Manual diagnosis is slow
- Many people ignore regular checkups
- No simple self-assessment tool
- Data patterns are not visually explained to users

4. Our Solution Fit
The Heart Disease Analysis System solves these limitations by providing:

✔ A Machine Learning model trained on 4500 real records  
✔ Web-based input system (Flask) for easy prediction  
✔ Risk assessment using 18 lifestyle + medical features  
✔ Tableau Dashboard for visual insights  
✔ Instant Yes/No result with no medical tests required  

5. Outcome
This solution perfectly fits the user’s need for:
- Early detection
- Awareness of risks
- Data-based decision-making
- Better lifestyle planning

The system is fast, accurate, free to use, and scientifically structured.
