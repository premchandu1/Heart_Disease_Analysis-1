BRAIN STORMING – HEART DISEASE ANALYSIS PROJECT

Dataset Size: 4500 records
Total Features: 18
Target Variable: HeartDisease (Yes / No)

1. What does the dataset contain?
   - Lifestyle factors: Smoking, Drinking, Physical Activity, Sleep Time
   - Medical history: Stroke, Diabetes, Asthma, Kidney Disease, SkinCancer
   - Physical metrics: BMI
   - Mental & physical health scores
   - Walking difficulty (DiffWalking)
   - Demographics: AgeCategory, Sex, Race
   - Overall General Health rating

2. What can we build from this dataset?
   ✔ A heart disease risk prediction model
   ✔ A dashboard showing:
       - Age group patterns
       - Whether smokers have higher risk
       - Impact of diabetes and stroke
       - BMI vs. Heart Disease correlation
       - Sleep time distribution
   ✔ A Flask web app for user risk prediction

3. Questions discussed during brainstorming:
   - Which factors contribute the most?
   - Does age or sex have strong influence?
   - Are smokers at higher risk than non-smokers?
   - Does BMI increase chances of heart disease?
   - Does poor mental/physical health indicate heart issues?

4. Possible challenges:
   - Categorical columns need encoding
   - Imbalanced classes (more "No" than "Yes")
   - Handling lifestyle features properly
   - Choosing correct ML model

5. Final brainstorming conclusion:
   Build a combined system:
   - ML Prediction Model
   - Tableau Visualization Dashboard
   - Flask Web Application
   - Complete Documentation
