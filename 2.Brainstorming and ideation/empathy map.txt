EMPATHY MAP — HEART DISEASE ANALYSIS PROJECT

USER TYPE:
Based on dataset attributes, the likely users are:
- Adults aged 18–80+
- Smokers or non-smokers
- Diabetic or non-diabetic patients
- People with physical or mental health issues
- Individuals checking their heart risk

WHAT THE USER THINKS:
- “My BMI is high — am I at risk?”
- “I have diabetes and stroke history — should I worry?”
- “I don’t exercise regularly. Is this dangerous?”

WHAT THE USER FEELS:
- Fear of developing heart disease
- Uncertainty about health condition
- Need for early warning or prediction
- Desire for simple and clear guidance

WHAT THE USER SAYS:
- “I want to know if my lifestyle affects my heart.”
- “Can I see which factors increase my risk?”
- “Is my sleep time affecting my health?”

WHAT THE USER DOES:
- Smokes or drinks (some users)
- Tracks BMI, sleep time, physical activity
- Consults doctors occasionally
- Searches online for prediction tools

USER PAINS (Based on dataset features):
- Poor general health ("GenHealth" categories)
- Difficulty walking (DiffWalking)
- High BMI
- Diabetes and stroke increasing risk
- Poor mental/physical health scores

USER GAINS:
✔ Easy understanding of risk factors  
✔ Simple prediction tool  
✔ Visual insights through dashboard  
✔ Early detection using ML  
✔ Awareness of lifestyle improvements
